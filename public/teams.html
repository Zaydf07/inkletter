Editor

Full access — create, edit, and export newsletters in the shared group.

Commenter

Can view everything and leave feedback and comments, but can't change the design.

Viewer

Read-only access — perfect for clients or stakeholders reviewing a draft.

Owner

Manages the team, billing, and who has access to which newsletters or groups.

📁

Shared groups

Organise newsletters into shared groups — a campaign, a client, a brand — and set access per group.

💬

Inline comments

Leave feedback right on a newsletter. Threads keep every decision in context, not lost in email.

🔐

Granular roles

Give each person exactly the access they need — edit, comment, or view — per newsletter or group.

🎨

Shared brand kit

One set of brand colours, fonts, and logos everyone on the team designs from automatically.
