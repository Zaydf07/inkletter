How AI credits work

Credits are only spent when AI does the writing or image work. Everything else — editing, templates, exporting — is unlimited on every plan.

~8 creditsGenerate a full newsletter
~2 creditsRewrite / refine a section
~5 creditsGenerate one AI image
0 creditsTemplates, editing & export

Questions

Is billing live?

Not yet — this pricing page is a preview of what's coming. During the beta, Inkletter is completely free to use with generous limits.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can keep editing, exporting, and sending existing newsletters — you just can't run new AI generations until your credits refresh next month (or you upgrade).

Do unused credits roll over?

Credits reset each month so everyone starts fresh. Plans are sized so most users never hit the limit.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. When billing goes live you'll be able to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time, and changes take effect on your next cycle.

How does team collaboration work?

On the Team plan you'll invite members and assign each a role — Editor (full edit), Commenter (leave feedback), or Viewer (read-only) — per newsletter or per shared group. See the preview →
